5 Steps to Free Your Parents from Your Heart

  1. Acknowledge the Pain: Accept that their mistakes have affected you. Denying it only makes healing harder.
  2. Understand Their Humanity: Your parents are imperfect people shaped by their own experiences and struggles.
  3. Release the Blame: Holding on to resentment only keeps you stuck. Let go of the need to punish them in your heart.
  4. Express Yourself: Write a letter (even if you don’t send it) or speak out your feelings in a safe space.
  5. Choose Peace Over Pain: Forgiveness doesn’t mean excusing their actions—it means freeing yourself from their hold.
